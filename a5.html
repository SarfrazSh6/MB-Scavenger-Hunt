A5 – Social Sciences Building

Your gut hosts a bustling social network of microbes — and drugs often crash the party. Around 66% of oral drugs are metabolized by at least one microbial strain. Proton Pump Inhibitors reduce microbial diversity and increase C. difficile infection risk, while Metformin alters microbiome composition in ways that affect glucose control. Microbes also influence cancer immunotherapy; taking PPIs or antibiotics during treatment can shorten overall survival by disrupting immune-supportive microbes.
